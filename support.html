Trailstation Support

Help, troubleshooting, and contact for the Trailstation app.

Trailstation is a time-synced audio player for iPhone and Apple Watch. Everything works on-device and fully offline — there is no Trailstation server and no account to manage.

The basics

How do I make a station?

Tap Build a Station, give it a name, add audio files (AAC, MP3, WAV, and more), set the crossfade if you want, and Save. Then open the station and tap Tune In to start playing.

Why do two phones playing the same station sound in sync?

A station loops against the wall clock, so every device works out the same spot in the loop from the current time. Two devices tuned into the same station land on the same moment without talking to each other. This relies on each device having an accurate clock — keep Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically turned on.

Sharing

How do I share a station with a friend?

Open the station, tap Share Station, and pick how to send it:

  • AirDrop or Email (file) — sends the single .trailstation file. AirDrop works fully offline and is best in person; email works anywhere. When your friend taps it (with Trailstation installed) it opens in the app.
  • Create iCloud Link — uploads the station to your own iCloud Drive and gives you a link you can paste into any message (including Messages). Your friend taps the link, downloads the file, and opens it in Trailstation. Needs you to be signed into iCloud.

You can also use Save to Files to keep or move the file yourself.

Messages isn’t offered as a way to send the file directly, because it can’t reliably open a custom file attachment on the receiving device — use an iCloud link (a tappable URL) or AirDrop instead.

The shared file doesn’t open in the app.

  • The recipient needs Trailstation installed first.
  • Send it via AirDrop and tap the notification to open it. (The Files app shows a blank preview for this file type by design — use the Share button there and choose Trailstation.)

Apple Watch

I sent stations to my watch but they haven’t arrived.

  • Confirm Trailstation is installed on the watch (open the Watch app on your iPhone to install it).
  • Transfers happen in the background and Apple deliberately delays large ones to protect the watch’s battery. Put your Apple Watch on its charger near your iPhone — that reliably lets queued transfers finish.
  • The Send button shows the current status (“Sending…”, “Synced…”, or why it can’t).

Managing stations

How do I edit or delete a station?

On the iPhone list, swipe a station left to Delete it, or right to Edit it (editing is available when the station isn’t playing). Deleting a station also removes the audio it used from the app. You can delete synced stations on the Apple Watch by swiping as well.
