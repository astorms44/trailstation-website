Trailstation

Time-synced trail radio for iPhone and Apple Watch.

Privacy Policy Support

Trailstation turns your own audio into a “station” that loops pinned to the wall clock. Because every device computes the same spot in the loop from the current time, two phones — or a whole group on a hike — that tune into the same station hear the same moment, to the second. No host, no setup, no network.

Build a station from audio you import, tune in, and share it with friends as a single file over AirDrop (or by email). It also runs on Apple Watch.

Fully offline. Trailstation makes no network connections, has no accounts, and collects no data. Everything stays on your device.
